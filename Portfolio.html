Humans are not the only
beings weaving through the
past, present, and future.

Yet, in our modern lives, we often forget this simple truth. The past held creatures that no longer exist; the present holds life that bridges the ages; and the future is meant to inherit the life surviving today.

However, humanity shatters this timeless cycle with its own hands. We now possess the power to dismantle species that have built their own worlds. Once extinct, these creatures linger only as remnants, fading away while leaving us with mere fragments to interpret.

On this stage created by man, behold the remnants of what our hands have erased! Cast in the role of 'Human' in this drama, how should we regard our fellow 'Animal' performers?

We express the project's intention through the element of 'Theater'. Theater, being an entirely virtual space created by humanity, provided the perfect medium to represent the animals reduced to remnants by human hands.

Earth allows us to breathe, but are we tightening the noose around its neck?

Environmental pollution often slips beneath our conscious radar, feeling eerily detached from our immediate reality.

his visual metaphor exposes that these scenes are unfolding inside us. It delivers a heavy message: humanity is intrinsic to nature, and our destruction of the environment is, ultimately, an act of self-destruction.

The digital world watches and manipulates us in myriad ways.

The digital world watches and manipulates us in myriad ways. We are controlled, much like scripted elements in a video game. One day, 'You' awaken to this reality and begin a desperate journey, jumping from world to world to break free.

Are you the master outside the glass, or the avatar inside?

To you, who yearns to leave everything behind.

Caught in the chaos of daily life, do you crave a distant sanctuary yet find yourself bound by time? If so, fill the white square with water and submerge 'Tonight' within it.

After a moment of silence, the mundane bathroom fades away. In its place, a universe expands, trees rise, and an ocean swells. Your journey begins right here.
